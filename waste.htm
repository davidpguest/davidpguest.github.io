Wastewatch

Please help us to keep the authorities informed of how you are affected by the Hollingdean Waste Transfer Station and Materials Recovery Facility.

Breaches of the EA's operating licence

On every occasion that you are significantly troubled by noise, odour or dust particles from The Waste Transfer Station &/or Materials Recovery Facility at Hollingdean Depot, phone The Environment Agency on 0800 807060. They will log your complaint.

Let The Round Hill Society know too

Whenever you encounter a problem, use this form (when switched on!) or email The Round Hill Society to file a quick report.

We will not share your name and address with other parties but would like to be able to contact you if we need more information.
